Route 3

Use marked bus stops at these locations:

  • Anderson Hall (eastbound)
  • 15th Ave & 4th St (eastbound)

This stop will be closed:

  • Pleasant & Jones Hall (eastbound)

When: All hours

Why: Long-term Mall of America Transit Center renovation

Buses will be relocated from Mall of America Transit Center to Mall of Americaâs Blue Lot, just south of the current bus platforms.

To access the temporary bus boarding platforms: Exit the indoor waiting area and turn right. Travel to the crosswalk that leads to the METRO Blue Line platform, turn left, and cross both tracks. Turn right and follow the cross walk to the temporary bus platform.

Where to find your bus

Gate A (Routes 54 and 415):

  • Gate A is at the far end of the first platform you reach when you enter the temporary transit center.

Gate B (Routes 5 and 515):

  • Gate B is behind Gate A on the first platform you reach when you enter the temporary transit center.

Gate C (Routes 444, 495, METRO Red Line):

  • To access Gate C, from the first platform, turn left and follow the crosswalk to the next platform.

Gate D (Routes 538, 539, 540, and 542):

  • To access Gate D, continue walking from Gate C to the next platform.
